Bulbul Hasan

I am a bilingual Montessori educator - guide, mentor, online trainer and facilitator of workshops who is passionate about working with children and adults in the field of Early Childhood Education. I trained through the Association Montessori Internationale and have my 3-6 year old Diploma certificate.

Working with Montessori education both with children and adults has been a journey for me and has given me the opportunity to live on three different continents and offer my work to children and adults across varied school communities around the world.

I deeply enjoy creating educational spaces for children that keep in mind their developmental needs (from birth through 6 years of age). I also love supporting teachers in their classroom environments as a mentor and have offered a variety of workshops to parents and teachers alike in schools in India, Mexico and Spain.

Montessori Guide

Pursuing a 3-6 AMI training was significant in my journey as an educator because it set me on a focused path of a discovery of children at this age and empowered me with skills required to create educational environments that helped them achieve the utmost potential of their growing years. .

Workshop facilitator

After more than fifteen years of day to day experience with children in Montessori and alternative schools around the world, I began to offer advisory services to schools and conduct Montessori themed workshops, enabling me to share my passion and offer tools for creating high functioning Montessori environments..

CONSULTANCY FOR SCHOOLS

A large part of my work with adults has been mentorship for schools which includes working alongside teachers, coordinators and directors of schools to guide and inspire them in their work with children.

Parent Educator

I have been invited by schools across the globe to conduct parent workshops that have given me the opportunity to connect with the essence of Montessori and relate it to a home setting.

Online trainer

I currently serve as Faculty for an online Montessori training program in India and I openly seek opportunities for shared learning online both in the English and Spanish speaking world.

Services Offered

All services can be offered online or in person at your school or institute

I provide Talks, Consultancy and Training workshops, broadly covering the following themes

TALKS

- Peace Education.
- INTRODUCTION TO THE MONTESSORI CLASSROOM
- CREATING A LANGUAGE ENVIRONMENT
- SETTING UP AN ART AREA
- NURTURING DISCIPLINE IN THE YOUNG CHILD
- NURTURING DISCIPLINE IN THE YOUNG CHILD

WORKSHOPS

-Introduction to Montessori philosophy and an integrated vision of the child.
-Deep dive into the different areas of a Montessori environment – Exercises of Practical Life and Grace and Courtesy, Sensorial, Language, Math, Culture, Art.
- Discipline strategies for young children and learning how to set respectful limits.
- Planning (weekly/monthly/yearly) for your 3-6 year old home or school environment and Record Keeping.
- Preparing an environment for the intellectual, social-emotional, creative, and holistic development of 3-6 year olds at home or at school.
- Preparation of the adult – parents and teachers to work with young children to bring out their full potential.
- The Art of Observation as the basis of our work with children.
- 8 exclusive sessions especially designed for parents.

CONSULTANCY/MENTORSHIP

- School visits
- Observation based concrete/constructive feedback keeping in mind the developmental needs and requirements of children aged 3-6 years.
- Accompanying teachers in the classroom for a period of time and providing in-the-moment feedback and guidance.
- Working with children for a period of time to provide real-time mentorship and inspiration.
- Guiding the preparation of the environment.
- Creating and executing parent events.

Montessori Consultancy Services

Sharing knowledge in Montessori Early Childhood Education.

After years of experience globally in the field of Montessori Early Childhood Education, what I offer in my continuing work with schools, children, teachers, and parents is a sharing of knowledge both in theory and practice. I look at my mentorship as a culmination of my varied experience and a help for schools and teachers that seek to elevate their understanding of the different aspects that create an integrated approach to child education. I also have the privilege of living and working on two continents, therefore being able to offer services bilingually in Spanish and English.
